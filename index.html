Profile

Ling Yin

Professor · Doctoral Supervisor

Ling Yin is a Professor and Doctoral Supervisor at the Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences. Her research interests include spatiotemporal data mining, GeoAI, human spatiotemporal behavior modeling, spatiotemporal epidemic modeling, and geographic information systems.

Drawing on urban big-data social sensing, geospatial intelligence, and high-performance computing, her team develops data-intelligence and efficient-computing methods for understanding disease transmission, human mobility, and traffic flow. The work supports public health, urban transportation, emergency management, and big-data services, and has been applied to the prevention and control of COVID-19, dengue fever, and influenza.

Research areas: Spatiotemporal Data Mining · GeoAI · Human Spatiotemporal Behavior Modeling · Spatiotemporal Epidemic Modeling · GIS
